Why Do We Canvass?

Canvassing in local neighborhoods (both at the doors and in high traffic areas) is the most effective way to talk with community members about the issues that matter to them. We need to let our neighbours know about our efforts and invite them to join us in the struggle for socialism in Scotland!

It is one of the most time-consuming ways of conducting a campaign, but it is also one of the most effective. If we come to somebodyâ€™s home, there is a much greater chance that he or she will remember us (compared to, say, reading our leaflet or an article about us in the local press).

Door-to-door campaigns are always worth doing even if we are inexperienced. It may be that someone is more willing to vote for a slightly lost-looking person who knocks on their door and presents his or her programme than for a completely anonymous leaflet left in their postbox. Remember that people/voters appreciate effort.
